Donation

Many students in title 1 schools go without warm clothing, food, or even parental supervision at home. As a teacher I see this daily and try to use any extra material or yarn I have to make hats for children that are in need. I have donated over 30 hats to children along with headbands to keep their ears warm. I am seeking to expand this my providing students who are in need with a new hat and heavier material to keep them warm. In order to make this a success I need your help. Donations would currently go to students who are in need in Las Vegas, Nevada but I would eventually like to expand this out so all communities are able to benefit from this especially those that are donating and would like to see this supporting their community.
